# Demo Script

## Title

SecureTheCloud Operational Intelligence Fabric: Governed AI Operations for Secure AI Banking

## Talk Track

This lab simulates a regulated banking operations environment where runtime telemetry, policy context, AI-assisted investigation, and human approval workflows are combined into a governed operational intelligence platform.

The AI assistant does not authorize or enforce actions. It summarizes evidence, correlates signals, explains policy context, and recommends a review path. Human approval is required before the recommendation is accepted, and every step is recorded in the audit chain.

## Demo Steps

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Show the operational overview.
3. Generate suspicious runtime event.
4. Open incident queue.
5. Select high-risk incident.
6. Show runtime evidence.
7. Show OPA policy decision.
8. Run AI investigation.
9. Show evidence-backed AI summary.
10. Record human approval decision.
11. Replay evidence timeline.
12. Show audit log.
13. Close with governance boundary explanation.
